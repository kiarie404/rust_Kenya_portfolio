ABOUT

Rust Kenya is a community of Devs and Companies who use Rust and associated tech stacks across different domains... embedded/IoT, web2 & web3, Fintech... wherever

WHY?

    1. Build a solid online presence of Kenyans building with/on rust, this encourages Rust's adoption.
    2. Build a united communication and interaction point among Kenyans interested in Rust
    3. Share rust content of multiple context. Promote #rustlang open source.
